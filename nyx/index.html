nyx
a night reader

read in the dark.

Paste the URL of a blog post or article. Nyx strips it bare, dims it deep, and renders it in grayscale — built for reading in bed without burning your retinas.

Note. Browsers block cross-origin requests, so Nyx routes fetches through a public CORS proxy (corsproxy.io). Nothing is stored — articles vanish when you leave the page.
